Share Your Intuition on a Phrase - And See What Returns

"Connect the dots, and see the river flow."

We want to know what it means to you.

How to Participate:

If you are from Britain or America: Please reply with one sentence on how you understand this phrase.

If you are from China: 请用中文告诉我们你对这句话的理解。如果能用成语来隐喻，则更为完美。

The Return (The Key Part): This is not a traditional reward. The insight and depth of your answer will determine the relevance and value of the content we send you next. A more thoughtful answer might unlock a more meaningful connection. Think of it as an intellectual key.

We are looking for true understanding, not just words. We await your intuition.
